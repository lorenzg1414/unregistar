Color Energy Guide

FORM P-06 — CHROMATIC ENERGY DIRECTIVE
What each color is reported to provide, and when to deploy it. Evidence: none has been located. The guide remains in effect.
Red

Confidence. Deploy for negotiations and other situations where retreat is not on the agenda.

Orange

Enthusiasm. Deploy sparingly. People will ask whether something happened.

Yellow

Luck, reportedly. Deploy on Mondays and other statistical low points.

Green

Renewal. Deploy at fresh starts, or to give the impression of one.

Blue

Calm. Deploy for difficult conversations. The other party need not be informed.

Violet

Intuition. Deploy when the data is insufficient and the decision is due anyway.

Pink

Goodwill. Deploy when gentleness is the strategy rather than the mood.

Black

Resolve. Deploy for boundaries, deadlines, and cities. The Bureau’s default.

White

Reset. Deploy on day one. Any day may be declared day one. No form is required.
