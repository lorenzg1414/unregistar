Color Energy Guide

FORM P-06 — CHROMATIC ENERGY DIRECTIVE
What each color channels, and when to wear it. Scientifically unverified. Anecdotally undefeated.
Red

Channels confidence, heat, first moves. Wear for: negotiations, stages, doors you have to walk through only once.

Orange

Channels play, appetite, saying yes. Wear for: reunions, road trips, second chances.

Yellow

Channels luck, morning energy, being seen kindly. Wear for: interviews, apologies, Mondays.

Green

Channels renewal, growth, quiet money. Wear for: fresh starts, difficult phone calls, spring.

Blue

Channels calm, clarity, being believed. Wear for: hard conversations, long days, telling the truth.

Violet

Channels intuition, strangeness, the third option. Wear for: decisions, full moons, art.

Pink

Channels tenderness, self-forgiveness, soft power. Wear for: birthdays, endings, being gentle on purpose.

Black

Channels resolve, edges, saying no. Wear for: boundaries, cities, getting things done.

White

Channels reset, blankness, beginning again. Wear for: day one. Any day can be day one.
